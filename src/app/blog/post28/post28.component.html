Content marketing is the best way to grow your social media following organically

  1. You must create valuable content for your audience. Content marketing is about giving value before asking for anything in return. It's not about selling directly on social media (that doesn't work) but building trust with people who are interested in what you have to say and will eventually buy from you. This means that you should be creating helpful, educational, or entertaining content for your target audience.
  2. Create an editorial calendar with a plan of action. You can use any scheduling tool to schedule posts ahead of time so that you don't have to monitor social media throughout the day or week constantly - but make sure it works best for your brand and team. The idea here again is consistency - when someone follows a brand on Instagram, for example, they want to see consistent, high-quality images/videos, etcetera. Otherwise, why would they follow in the first place? This creates trust over time which leads back into step 1 above, where people start talking positively about brands they like (positive word-of-mouth referrals).
